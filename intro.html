Taylor Swift Lyrics Analysis

Home Intro

Taylor Swift is one of the most famous popstars of this era. She is known for her poetic songwriting skills. Currently, she has seven studio albums: Taylor Swift (2006), Fearless (2008), Speak Now (2010), Red (2012), 1989 (2014), Reputation (2017), Lover (2019).

Growing up listening to Taylor Swift, I really enjoy her songwriting that really captures the feelings of youth. This project uses Python and SQL to analyze her lyrics and uses HTML, CSS and JavaScript for the interface.

Feel free to check out myprocessused for the analysis.
